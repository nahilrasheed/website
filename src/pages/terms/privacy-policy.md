## 1. Information Collection

This website uses analytics tools such as **PostHog** and **Umami** to better understand user behavior and improve the site experience.
As part of this, we may collect the following information:

- IP addresses
- Device and browser information
- Pages visited, links clicked, and time spent on the site
- Geolocation data (based on IP address)

## 2. Use of Information

The information collected is strictly used for analytical purposes to help us understand which articles are most popular and how users interact with the site. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

## 3. Cookies

Our analytics tools, such as PostHog, may place cookies on your device to track your sessions across visits. You can control or disable these cookies via your browser settings.

## 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out via the email listed on the homepage.
